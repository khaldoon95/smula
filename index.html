How the score works
Three things, one number
Fullness
How filling is this food per calorie?
35% of score
Protein
Protein content and quality
35% of score
Nourishment
Vitamins, minerals, micronutrients
30% of score
Built for eating well without overthinking it — foods that fill you up, support muscle, and cover your micronutrients. It's not a universal health score. Olive oil scores lower here, not because it's unhealthy, but because it serves a different purpose.

Same food, different goal
Your goal changes the rankings
✂ Lose weight
Fullness matters most. Foods that fill you up on fewer calories rank higher.
◎ Eat well
Balanced weights across fullness, protein, and nutrition. Good default for most.
▲ Build muscle
Calorie-dense, protein-rich foods rank higher. Fullness works against you here.
Switching goal recalculates every score and shifts some foods. Salmon goes Essential on Build muscle. Boiled potato drops on Build muscle. Rice goes up. Same food, different value depending on what you're trying to do.
How to use it
Simple by design
Tap any food to see its note and why it ranked where it did.
Tap + More for full macros, key micronutrients, and the reasoning behind the score.
Add to shop saves foods to your shopping list — it remembers between sessions.
Build a plate — add foods, pick portions (S/M/L), and get a meal score with flags for what's missing or overdone.
